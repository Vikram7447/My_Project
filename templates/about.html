About Us

Welcome to the Mental Health Journal & Chatbot project. This platform is designed to support your emotional well-being by offering:

  • 📝 A safe space to write and reflect through journaling.
  • 🤖 An empathetic AI chatbot that listens and responds supportively.
  • 📊 Mood detection powered by GoEmotions to help you track your emotions.
  • 🔒 Secure login & encryption to protect your personal entries.

Our goal is to encourage mindfulness, reduce stress, and promote emotional health. Remember, you are not alone — journaling and talking can make a difference. 💙
